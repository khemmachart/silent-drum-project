About Us

The Background of our website

Nowaday, There are several career that make people has many ways and occasion to choose the jobs. Although each career has many possible factors to realize, one of the most important factor is salary. The salary of each career is constantly changing day by day, so it unpredictable for long team decision making. Therefore, our project is to create for analysis and generate reliable data from career salary infomation to support decision making.

The objective of out website

First of all, it can show the data which help people determine for their job. Secondly, our website can presents the change rate of income of each career per year. Then analysis and show data of income for 10 years back (since 2006 to 2014) In addition, it can demonstrate the comparison and average income of each career. Finally, this website can show the overtime income of each career.
